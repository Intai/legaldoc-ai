Non-Disclosure Agreement

This Non-Disclosure Agreement (the “Agreement”) is entered into as of March 27, 2026 (the “Effective Date”), by and between:

Disclosing Party: Acme Corporation, a Delaware corporation, with its principal office at 123 Innovation Drive, San Francisco, CA 94105.

Receiving Party: Widget Industries LLC, a California limited liability company, with its principal office at 456 Technology Boulevard, Palo Alto, CA 94301.

Article I — Definitions

1.1 “Confidential Information” means any and all non-public information, whether written, oral, electronic, or visual, disclosed by either party to the other, including but not limited to: trade secrets, business plans, financial information, technical data, product designs, customer lists, and marketing strategies.
1.2 “Purpose” means the evaluation and negotiation of a potential business relationship between the parties, including the exchange of information necessary to assess mutual compatibility and commercial viability.

Article II — Obligations

2.1 The Receiving Party shall hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and shall not disclose such information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party.
2.2 The Receiving Party shall use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose and shall not use such information for any other purpose, including but not limited to competitive advantage or commercial exploitation.
2.3 The Receiving Party shall protect the Confidential Information using the same degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care.

Article III — Term

3.1 This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of two (2) years from the Effective Date, unless terminated earlier by mutual written agreement of the parties.
